About Us

The SWIZEN GODERD FOUNDATION is committed to helping orphans, promoting talents, supporting the needy, delivering charity, and defending human rights across Uganda. Founded to serve the Bundibugyo District and operate nationally, our goal is to create sustainable programs that educate, uplift and protect children and families in need.

Our Story

Started by local community leaders, we've grown into a nationally recognized foundation working with schools, community groups, and volunteers to provide consistent care, education, and advocacy.

Our Team

We work with dedicated staff and volunteers across Uganda who manage programs in education, talent development, health outreach, and rights advocacy.
